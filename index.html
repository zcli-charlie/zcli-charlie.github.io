Zuchao Li 李祖超

Associate Professor

School of Artificial Intelligence, Wuhan University  ·  Wuhan 430072, China

I am an Associate Professor at Wuhan University. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Hai Zhao. I have also visited the National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT), Japan, as a Limited Technical Researcher.

My research focuses on natural language processing and language-centric AI, with particular interests in large language models, efficient AI inference, and multimodal understanding. I am actively recruiting motivated master/PhD students and undergraduate interns.
