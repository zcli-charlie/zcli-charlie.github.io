Biography

I am an Associate Professor at Wuhan University. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Hai Zhao. I have visited the National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT), Japan, as a Limited Technical Researcher, hosted by Doc. Masao Utiyama and Doc. Eiichiro Sumita.

My current research centers on natural language processing , language-centric AI, including large language models (LLMs), multimodal large models, and efficient AI. I am interested in principled modeling, learning, and reasoning with language and multimodal signals, and in making foundation models more efficient and deployable in real-world settings.

Openings: I am actively recruiting master/PhD students and undergraduate interns. Please contact me with your detailed CV.
