Biography

I am now a Associate Researcher at Wuhan University. I got my PhD degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Hai Zhao. I have visited National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT) as Limited Technical Researcher, Japan, hosted by Doc. Masao Utiyama and Doc. Eiichiro Sumita.

My research interests including the language sequence modeling, linguistic structure parsing, and language representation learning from various types of data (unlabeled or noisy data, structured data like tree, graph, etc.). Specifically, I focus on theoretical and algorithmic approaches for syntax/semantic parsing, self-supervised/weakly supervised learning and structure learning.

I am actively recruiting master students and undergraduate interns! Welcome to contact me with your detailed CV in advance!
